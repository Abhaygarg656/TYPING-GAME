## ğŸ® Gameplay Overview

In ** type Mission**, words fall from the top of the screen like enemy spacecraft. Your mission is to type the letters of each word to fire lasers and destroy them before they reach your base at the bottom.

### Key Features:
* **Dynamic Difficulty**: The game features a wave system where the speed and frequency of words increase as your score grows.
* **Visual Effects**: Includes neon-style graphics, particle explosions, and laser beam animations for an immersive arcade feel.
* **Retro Audio**: Integrated with the Web Audio API to provide classic 8-bit sound effects for hits, explosions, and game-over sequences.
* **Health System**: You start with 100% health. Each word that bypasses your defense reduces your health by 20%.

## ğŸ•¹ï¸ How to Play

1.  **Launch**: Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
2.  **Start**: Click the **START MISSION** button on the main menu.
3.  **Defend**: Type the letters of the words as they appear on the screen. 
    * Once you start typing a word, you are "locked-on" to that target until it is destroyed.
4.  **Advance**: Reach higher scores to trigger new waves with faster enemies.
5.  **Game Over**: The mission fails if your health bar reaches 0%.
